# SidekickWriter — The AI-Powered Book Writing Platform That Actually Lets You Write

## What Is SidekickWriter?

SidekickWriter (sidekickwriter.com) is a full-featured, AI-powered book writing and generation platform designed for authors, researchers, entrepreneurs, and creators who want to turn ideas into complete, structured books — without surrendering creative control to a machine. Unlike generic AI writing tools that spit out soulless walls of text, SidekickWriter acts as an intelligent co-pilot: it suggests, refines, researches, and generates — but the author stays in the driver's seat at every step.

SidekickWriter supports Fiction, Non-Fiction, Self-Help & Wellness, Business & Professional, and Academic book types — each with tailored AI prompts, generation configs, and workflows optimized for that genre.

## Core Features

### 1. End-to-End Book Creation Workflow

SidekickWriter provides a structured, step-by-step process to go from a blank idea to a finished, exportable book:

1. **Main Idea Generation** — Start with a rough concept. The AI can generate, suggest, or refine your main idea. You can also hit "Random Idea" for inspiration.
2. **Writing Style Selection** — Choose from predefined writing styles or define a completely custom one. The AI adapts its voice to match.
3. **Character Development (Fiction)** — Add characters with names, backgrounds, motivations, and personality traits. The AI can suggest or refine any of these fields individually.
4. **Outline Generation** — Generate a full chapter-by-chapter outline from your idea, or write your own and let the AI refine it into a tighter structure.
5. **Chapter Descriptions** — Each chapter gets a detailed description that guides generation. AI can suggest or refine per chapter, with awareness of all previous chapters for continuity.
6. **Full Book Generation** — Generate the entire book or individual chapters with real-time streaming text. Watch your book come to life in real time.
7. **Inline Editing** — Every chapter is fully editable after generation. Make changes, save instantly, and regenerate specific chapters without losing the rest.

### 2. Two Generation Modes: Guided and Pro

- **Guided Mode** — Structured prompts and guardrails. Best for first-time authors or anyone who wants more AI assistance in shaping the narrative.
- **Pro Mode** — Minimal constraints. The AI follows your descriptions closely with less intervention. For experienced writers who know exactly what they want.

### 3. AI Research Engine with Citations

SidekickWriter has a built-in deep research system that goes beyond simple AI generation:

- **Web Search Integration** — Finds real sources from internet and academic databases.
- **AI Summarization** — Fetches and summarizes source content with AI.
- **Citation Styles** — Supports APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE, and more. Citations are woven directly into the research output.
- **Streaming Research** — Research results stream in real time so you can watch the process unfold.
- **Cancellable** — Long research tasks can be cancelled mid-stream.

This is especially powerful for **Academic** and **Non-Fiction** book types where factual accuracy and proper citations matter.
