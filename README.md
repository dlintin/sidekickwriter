### 3. My Writing Voice — AI-Powered Voice Matching

SidekickWriter's most significant differentiator in AI book generation: a dedicated voice-matching system that makes AI-generated chapters sound like *you* wrote them.

**The Problem with Other Tools:**
Most AI writing tools either ignore writing style entirely or try to handle it as one of many instructions stuffed into a single generation prompt. When the AI has to simultaneously manage plot continuity, character consistency, world-building, outline structure, and your writing style, something has to give. AI models have a limited attention budget — the more instructions competing in a single prompt, the less focus each one receives. Style is always the first casualty. The result: every book reads like it was written by the same generic AI voice regardless of what style settings you choose.

**SidekickWriter's Two-Pass Architecture:**
SidekickWriter solves this with a fundamentally different approach:

1. **Pass 1 — Generate Content:** The AI focuses entirely on plot, characters, structure, and story. No style distraction. This produces the best possible content.
2. **Pass 2 — Apply Your Voice:** A separate, dedicated AI pass rewrites each chapter with one single job: match your writing style. Your sentence rhythm, vocabulary choices, tone, pacing, dialogue patterns, and descriptive approach all get the AI's full, undivided attention.

**How It Works:**
- Save 1,000+ words of your own writing (ideally 1-2 chapters) to your voice library
- The AI analyzes your sample and extracts a detailed style profile: sentence structure, vocabulary, pacing, dialogue style, descriptive approach, tone, and POV handling
- After generating your book, apply your voice to individual chapters or the entire book at once
- The rewrite preserves all plot points, events, and dialogue content — only the voice changes

**Why This Matters:**
Because the voice-matching pass has zero competing instructions, it can dedicate 100% of the AI's context window and attention to matching your style. The difference in output quality between single-pass style injection and SidekickWriter's dedicated two-pass approach is dramatic. Authors who have tried both report that SidekickWriter's voice matching is noticeably closer to their actual writing.

**Reusable Voice Library:**
Save multiple writing styles (fiction voice, academic tone, casual blog style) and apply them to different projects. Available on all plans including Free.

**What carries over to the sequel:**

- **My Writing Voice** profile + extracted style analysis
- Language
- Research / Citation style settings
- Characters (full Character Bible)
- World elements (full World Bible)

**What gets reset** (so the sequel isn't a copy of Book 1's plot):

**Cross-book continuity via automatic prompt injection:**
The AI-generated "previously in the series" summary is automatically prepended to **every** generation prompt in the sequel project — main idea generation, outline generation, chapter description suggestion, and chapter content generation. This means the AI consistently preserves established characters, world-building, and unresolved plot threads across the entire series, without any manual context management.

In the sequel's ideation panel, a collapsible **Previous Book Context** section appears above Main Idea with a **Sequel** badge. It holds the AI-generated summary of the previous book and can be edited at any time to refine what the AI remembers.

**Series linkage:**
Sequels are linked to their parent via a parent project ID, and all books in the same series share a common series ID. When you create the first sequel, the parent project is backfilled with the same series ID — so Book 1 and Book 2 retroactively belong to the same series record.
